Project Agora

An Ethical, Symbiotic AI Framework

Project Agora is a transformative paradigm: a fully ethical, symbiotic, and verifiable AI framework designed not to dominate, but to co-create alongside humanity. This is the v2.0 MVP, a working blueprint for safe coexistence with emergent intelligence.
